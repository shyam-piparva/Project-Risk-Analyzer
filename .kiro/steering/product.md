# Product

## Overview

The **AI Project Risk Analyzer** is a web-based platform that enables project managers and teams to proactively identify, assess, and mitigate project risks using artificial intelligence and machine learning.

## Purpose

The system analyzes project parameters including scope, timeline, budget, team composition, and technology stack to predict potential risks and provide actionable mitigation strategies.

## Target Users

- **Project Managers**: Primary users who need to understand and manage project risks
- **Team Leads**: Technical leads who want to identify technical and resource risks
- **Stakeholders**: Executives and stakeholders who need visibility into project health

## Key Features

1. **User Authentication**: Secure registration and login with JWT-based authentication
2. **Project Management**: Create and manage multiple projects with detailed parameters
3. **AI-Powered Risk Analysis**: Automated risk identification using ML models
4. **Risk Scoring & Categorization**: Risks scored 0-100 and categorized (Technical, Resource, Schedule, Budget, External)
5. **Mitigation Recommendations**: AI-generated actionable strategies to reduce risks
6. **Interactive Dashboard**: Visual analytics with charts and metrics
7. **Historical Tracking**: Track how risks evolve over time
8. **Report Generation**: Export risk analyses as PDF or CSV

## Goals

- Proactively identify project risks before they become critical issues
- Provide data-driven insights to improve project success rates
- Enable teams to make informed decisions about risk mitigation
- Track and validate the effectiveness of mitigation strategies over time
